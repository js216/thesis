\section{Conclusion}

In this thesis, I have presented the progress of the \CENTREX\ experiment
towards its goal of providing an improved measurement of the upper bound on the
Schiff moment in thallium fluoride. The main contribution of this thesis work
has been to construct the beam source and the magnetic field setup in the form
of the magnetic shield with shim coils.
